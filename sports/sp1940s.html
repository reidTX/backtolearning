The NFL began to gain more attention

The NFL games were gaining attention as the games were now able to be broadcasted over the radio and television.

Professional Basketball

Professional basketball made its appearance as the NBA, although the sport had been around for a few decades and appeared in the olympics it finally became a professional sport in 1946.

Jackie Robison

Jackie Robison was the first African American athlete to play major league baseball when he took the field for the Brooklyn dodgers in 1947. He went down in history as one of the most exciting and talented players in history.
