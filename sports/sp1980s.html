Michael Jordan

Micheal Jordan is an infamous basketball player who gained popularity while he was in college as when he was a freshman, he was a member of the Tar Heels' national championship team in 1982. Jordan joined the Bulls in 1984 as the third overall draft pick and quickly emerged as a league star, entertaining crowds with his prolific scoring while gaining a reputation as one of the best defensive players.

Sidd Finch

Sidd Finch was a baseball player who seemingly came out of left field. His talent was unrefined but unseen; he was signed to the New York Mets and the media had a field day trying to catch a glimpse of this athletic wonder.
