COVID

COVID was a global pandemic that was an infectious disease caused by the SARS virus. Individuals that were effected began to have respiratory infections and in some cases, especially with those who had previous health problems or who were ill, led to death.

CARES Act

The Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security (CARES) Act was a $2.2 trillion economic stimulus bill passed by Congress in March 2020 to address the economic impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. The CARES Act provided direct assistance to American families, workers, small businesses, and industries.
