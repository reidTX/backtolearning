Blockbuster franchise rise

Under Huizenga's leadership, Blockbuster expanded rapidly through both corporate-owned stores and franchising. By the early 1990s, Blockbuster had thousands of stores across the United States and internationally. 1990: Blockbuster acquires major competitors and expands into new markets.

IMAX films

Then, in 2000, in a joint venture with Disney, IMAX released the first theatrical IMAX film: Disney’s “Fantasia 2000.

As computer technology evolves and expands, it has become possible to overcome the technical difficulties of the past and convert a number of theatrical releases to the IMAX format. IMAX DMR (Digital Media Remastering) technology helps to facilitate this process of conversion. The last several years have seen the use of IMAX cameras to shoots some scenes in mainstream movies.
