COVID

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Most people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention. Older people and those with underlying medical conditions like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, or cancer are more likely to develop serious illness. Anyone can get sick with COVID-19 and become seriously ill or die at any age.

Speed up photosynthesis

Enhanced photosynthesis techniques are theoretical geoengineering technologies based on the idea that plants and algae, particularly trees and crops like rice, wheat and cotton, can be genetically modified to absorb and metabolise and therefore store more carbon dioxide.
